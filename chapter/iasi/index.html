Iasi Kickstart

Map Date/Time: 2016-01-21 07:00pm Location: Utopia Cafe - Utopia, Strada Banu Numărul 8, Bloc 8, Iași, Romania, Iasi

With the New Year it's also time for the first "Papers We Love" Iasi meetup! We will hold it on January the 21st, from 7 PM at Utopia Cafe. Please RSVP as soon as possible, such that we have an idea about how many of you will join us.

The schedule for this meetup is:

19:00 - 19:10: What "Papers We Love" is and what the Iasi chapter aims at

19:10 - 20:00: "TAO: Facebook’s Distributed Data Store for the Social Graph" (USENIX13, http://j.mp/1Z2KvDd)

Both presentations will be held by Adrian-Tudor Panescu (only this time though). After the presentations you are free to discuss about the paper, Papers We Love in general or anything else. See you there!
